About This Project

This platform is a proof-of-concept exploring the applications of artificial intelligence in image processing. The project investigates AI-driven techniques for analyzing and enhancing various types of images, including medical imaging, scientific visualizations, and general image-based data.

The primary objective is to experiment with different AI models, evaluate their potential impact, and understand their limitations in real-world applications. While this is not a production-ready tool, it serves as a research initiative to explore the feasibility of AI in image analysis.
