PairGAN for Battery Analysis

PairGAN is a specialized form of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) designed to learn paired relationships between input and output data. In the context of battery analysis, PairGAN can be applied to:

  • Detect defects such as voids, cracks, or delamination in CT scan images of batteries.
  • Enhance the quality of low-resolution scans by generating high-resolution counterparts.
  • Analyze material composition by learning paired relationships between scanned and annotated images.

This page provides insights into how PairGAN can revolutionize battery diagnostics using advanced image processing techniques.
